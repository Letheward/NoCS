## Chord Progression Analyze using PI

Major 251: `+ 2 => + 5 => + 8`

`{2 5 9 0}`: `4 - 5 + 3 = + 2`

`{7 11 2 5}`: `5 + 1 + 4 - 5 = + 5`

`{0 4 7 11}`: `0 + 2 + 5 + 1 = + 8`

Minor 251:`- 3 => + 5 => 0`

`{2 5 8 0}`:`4 - 5 - 2 = - 3`

`{7 11 2 5}`:`5 + 1 + 4 - 5 = + 5`

`{0 3 7 10}`:`- 3 + 5 - 2 = 0`

### PI Equivalent Progression

`{2 9 4 1}`:`4 + 3 + 2 -1 = + 8`
